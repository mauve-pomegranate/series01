Event Timeline

Book 1: Fate and Foxfire

Yume's Disappearance

Event 1

Yume Minamoto disappears after a cryptic tarot reading with Ichiro Kobayashi in Watanabe Park.

The Investigation Begins

Event 2 & 3

Bowie is tasked with finding Yume. He and Mizuki, who is on her own investigation, form an uneasy alliance.

Corporate Espionage

Event 4

Zenko operative Shikako infiltrates Kagami Tenko R&D, uncovering illegal experiments and corporate malfeasance.

The Ikanajima Incident

Event 5, 6, 7

The group converges on Ikanajima Island. Bowie enters the Kozyrev Mirror, fracturing reality and prompting the Celestial Council to deploy a stasis bubble.

Hisoka Awakens

Event 8

While trapped in the Æther, Mizuki undergoes trials and awakens her latent kitsune powers, embracing the name Hisoka.

Climax & Sacrifice

Event 9 & 10

Professor Tajima sacrifices himself to disrupt Project Tsunagari. Yume merges with Sakura's essence, becoming a new, more powerful being.

Aftermath

Event 11

To protect him from the Yako Syndicate and the consequences of the event, Hisoka wipes Bowie's memory of the entire affair.
